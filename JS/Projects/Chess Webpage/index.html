GERMAN KNIGHT

CHECK THE

FIGURES

The Marengo chess set is a handcrafted masterpiece named after Napoleon’s Arabian stallion. It features a unique Staunton design with an intricately carved knight and a coherent pattern from pawn to king. The set is made from ebony and boxwood, with heavily weighted pieces for stability. The king stands at 4.25" tall with a unique ornate finial. The set includes an extra queen for each color and a certificate of authenticity. It’s perfect for collectors or those seeking a special chess set.
